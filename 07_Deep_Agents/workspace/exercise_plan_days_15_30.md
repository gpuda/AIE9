# 30-Day Wellness Challenge (Days 15-30)  
## Overview  
This modified exercise plan focuses on increasing mobility, reducing intensity for strength training, and incorporating recovery-focused sessions, ensuring support for knee health while maintaining overall fitness, particularly for hiking.  

---  
### Weekly Breakdown  
#### Week 3 (Days 15-21)  
- **Monday - Mobility & Active Recovery**  
  - **Warm-Up (5 minutes)**:  
    - March in place: 1 min  
    - Gentle knee circles: 1 min  
    - Ankle swings: 1 min  
    - Arm circles: 1 min  
    - Torso twists: 1 min  
  - **Mobility & Flexibility (15 minutes)**:  
    - Cat-Cow: 2 sets of 8-10 reps  
    - Seated hamstring stretch: 30 seconds each side  
    - Standing quadriceps stretch: 30 seconds each side  
    - Figure four stretch: 30 seconds each side  
    - IT band stretch: 30 seconds each side  
  - **Cool Down (5 minutes)**:  
    - Deep breathing and relaxation poses  

- **Tuesday - Low-Impact Strength**:  
  - **Bodyweight Exercises (20 minutes)**:  
    - Wall sits: 3 sets of 15-30 seconds  
    - Seated leg raises: 3 sets of 10 reps each leg  
    - Glute bridges: 3 sets of 10-15 reps  
    - Resistance band lateral leg raises: 3 sets of 10 reps each leg  
  - **Cool Down (10 minutes)**:  
    - Full body stretch focusing on legs and back  

- **Wednesday - Hiking-Specific Conditioning & Mobility**:  
  - **Low-Impact Cardio (15 minutes)**: Choose one  
    - Bike (stationary or outdoor)  
    - Swimming  
    - Elliptical machine  
  - **Mobility Work (15 minutes)**:  
    - Dynamic lunges with torso rotation: 2 sets of 8-10 reps  
    - Standing calf raises: 3 sets of 10-15 reps  
    - Hip flexor stretch: 30 seconds each side  

- **Thursday - Recovery & Gentle Movement**:  
  - **Gentle Yoga or Stretching (30 minutes)** focusing on flexibility and relaxation  

- **Friday - Mobility & Strength Combination**:  
  - **Mobility Warm-Up**  
  - **Strength Exercises (20 minutes)**:  
    - Chair squats: 3 sets of 10 reps  
    - Step-ups (low step): 3 sets of 8-10 reps each leg  
    - Side-lying leg raises: 3 sets of 10 reps each leg  
  - **Cool Down (5 minutes)**:  
    - Full body stretching routine  

- **Saturday - Active Recovery**:  
  - Take a light hike, focusing on enjoying the scenery and gentle movement.  

- **Sunday - Rest and Reflect**:  
  - Focus on hydration and nutrition; prepare for the upcoming week.  

---  
#### Week 4 (Days 22-30)  
- Same structure as Week 3, but gradually increase repetitions or duration by 1-2 reps or seconds where appropriate, adjusting based on comfort and feedback regarding knee sensitivity.  

---  
### Recovery-Focused Sessions  
- Aim for at least one dedicated recovery session every week, incorporating foam rolling or deep tissue massage if accessible.  

---  
### Checklist  
- Daily reminders for morning routine completion, hydration, and tracking knee responsiveness to exercises.  

---  
This plan is designed to promote recovery, while progressively improving strength, flexibility, and mobility to support future hiking goals.
