# Goran's 30-Day Vegetarian Nutrition Guide

## User Profile
- Vegetarian diet
- Active lifestyle with hiking goals
- Building strength and endurance
- Morning exercise preference
- Dealing with knee pain (anti-inflammatory focus beneficial)

## Anti-Inflammatory Vegetarian Foods
### Fruits
- Berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
- Cherries (especially tart cherries)
- Oranges and citrus fruits
- Pineapple
- Pomegranate

### Vegetables
- Leafy greens (spinach, kale, arugula)
- Broccoli and cruciferous vegetables
- Carrots and sweet potatoes
- Bell peppers
- Beets

### Healthy Fats
- Avocados
- Nuts (walnuts, almonds, pistachios)
- Seeds (chia, flax, hemp)
- Olive oil (extra virgin)
- Coconut oil

### Whole Grains
- Quinoa
- Brown rice
- Oats
- Barley
- Buckwheat

### Legumes
- Lentils (red, green, black)
- Chickpeas
- Black beans
- Kidney beans
- White beans

### Herbs & Spices
- Turmeric
- Ginger
- Garlic
- Cinnamon
- Green tea

## Pre and Post-Workout Nutrition

### Pre-Workout (30-60 minutes before, 6:00-6:30 AM)
**Quick Energy Options:**
- Banana with 1 tbsp almond butter
- Greek yogurt with honey and berries
- Small bowl of oatmeal with fruit
- Slice of whole grain toast with avocado

### Post-Workout (within 30 minutes, ~8:00 AM)
**Recovery Options:**
- Smoothie with protein powder, banana, and spinach
- Quinoa bowl with chickpeas and vegetables
- Greek yogurt with nuts and fruit
- Cottage cheese with pineapple

## Daily Meal Structure for Early Risers
- **5:30 AM:** Wake up, hydrate
- **6:00 AM:** Pre-workout snack
- **6:30-7:00 AM:** Exercise
- **8:00 AM:** Post-workout breakfast
- **12:00 PM:** Lunch
- **3:00 PM:** Afternoon snack
- **6:30 PM:** Dinner
- **8:30 PM:** Optional evening snack (if hungry)

## Sample Weekly Meal Plan

### Monday
- **Breakfast:** Overnight oats with chia seeds, berries, and almond milk
- **Lunch:** Quinoa bowl with black beans, avocado, and mixed vegetables
- **Dinner:** Stir-fried tofu with broccoli, carrots, and brown rice
- **Snacks:** Hummus with carrot sticks, handful of walnuts

### Tuesday
- **Breakfast:** Greek yogurt with granola and fresh fruit
- **Lunch:** Lentil soup with whole grain bread
- **Dinner:** Chickpea curry with quinoa
- **Snacks:** Apple with almond butter, herbal tea

### Wednesday
- **Breakfast:** Smoothie with spinach, banana, berries, and protein powder
- **Lunch:** Buddha bowl with roasted vegetables and tahini dressing
- **Dinner:** Black bean and sweet potato tacos
- **Snacks:** Trail mix with nuts and dried fruit

## Hydration Guidelines
- **Daily Goal:** 8-10 cups (2-2.5 liters) of water
- **Morning:** 16-20 oz upon waking
- **Pre-workout:** 8 oz
- **During workout:** Sip as needed
- **Post-workout:** 16-20 oz
- **Throughout day:** Consistent sipping

## Supplement Considerations for Vegetarians
- **Vitamin B12:** Essential supplement (2.4 mcg daily)
- **Omega-3:** Algae-based supplement (if not eating enough walnuts/flax)
- **Vitamin D:** Especially important for bone health
- **Protein powder:** Plant-based option for post-workout recovery
- **Iron:** If levels are low (best absorbed with vitamin C)
