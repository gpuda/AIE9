30-Day Joint-Friendly Exercise Program for User Goran

USER PROFILE:
- Enjoys hiking and outdoor activities.
- Has occasional knee pain requiring joint-friendly exercises.
- Goals: Stay active without aggravating knee, build strength and endurance for hiking.
- Constraints: 30 minutes per day on weekdays, prefers morning activity.
- Fitness level: Appears active but needs knee-safe modifications.

### PROGRAM STRUCTURE:
The program is divided into four weeks, focusing on progressively increasing strength and endurance for hiking while maintaining knee safety.

#### Week 1: Foundation Building
- **Warm-up Routine (5 mins):**
  - Gentle leg swings (front-to-back & side-to-side)
  - Arm circles
  - Torso twists
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Bodyweight squats (to chair level) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  - Wall push-ups - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  - Clamshells - 3 sets of 10-15 reps per side
  - Marching in place - 5 mins (low knee lift)
- **Cool-down (5 mins):**
  - Gentle stretching (hamstrings, quads, hips, arms)

#### Week 2: Building Strength
- **Warm-up Routine (5 mins):** (same as Week 1)
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Step-ups (onto a low step, alternate legs) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  - Seated leg lifts - 3 sets of 10-15 reps per leg
  - Side leg raises - 3 sets of 10-15 reps per leg
  - Walking (outdoors or in place) â€“ 10 mins at a comfortable pace
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** (same as Week 1)

#### Week 3: Endurance & Balance
- **Warm-up Routine (5 mins):** (same as Week 1)
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Glute bridges - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  - Modified lunge (only lowering half-way) - 3 sets of 5-10 reps per leg
  - Heel-to-toe walk for balance - 5 mins
  - Low-impact cardio (walking, cycling, or swimming) - 10 mins
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** (same as Week 1)

#### Week 4: Integration & Outdoor Activities
- **Warm-up Routine (5 mins):** (same as Week 1)
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Combination of exercises from previous weeks (select favorites)
  - Incorporate a 15-min outdoor walk on a flat surface (when possible)
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** (same as Week 1)

### KNEE PAIN MODIFICATION GUIDELINES:
- On days with knee pain, focus on low-impact cardio and avoid any exercises causing discomfort.
- Replace squats and lunges with seated leg lifts or gentle stretches.
- Consult a physical therapist for personalized knee-safe modifications if pain persists.

### EQUIPMENT NEEDS:
- Light dumbbells (optional)
- Resistance bands (optional)
- A sturdy chair
- A low step (for step-ups)

### PROGRESS TRACKING SUGGESTIONS:
- Keep a journal of daily exercises completed and rate knee pain levels.
- Track walking distances or times during outdoor activities.
- Set specific hiking goals (distance, elevation) to achieve by the end of 30 days.

### NOTES:
- Prioritize enjoyment and listen to your body throughout the program. Make modifications as needed.
