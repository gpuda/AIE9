# Goran's Week 1 Action Plan - 30-Day Wellness Challenge

## Week 1 Goals
- Establish consistent morning exercise routine (foundation building)
- Implement sleep hygiene and evening routine
- Start anti-inflammatory vegetarian nutrition plan
- Begin tracking knee pain and sleep quality

## Daily Schedule Template

### 5:30 AM - Morning Routine
- **Wake up & hydrate:** Glass of water with lemon
- **Brief mindfulness:** 5-minute breath awareness meditation
- **Gentle stretching:** Light movement to wake up the body

### 6:00 AM - Pre-Workout Fuel
**Choose one:**
- Banana with 1 tbsp almond butter
- Small bowl of oatmeal with berries
- Greek yogurt with honey

### 6:30-7:00 AM - Exercise (Week 1 Foundation)
**Warm-up (5 minutes):**
- Gentle leg swings (10 each direction)
- Arm circles (10 forward, 10 backward)
- Torso twists (10 each direction)

**Workout (20 minutes):**
- Modified bodyweight squats (10-15 reps)
- Wall push-ups (10-15 reps)
- Clamshells for hip stability (10 each side)
- Marching in place (2-3 minutes)
- Rest between exercises as needed

**Cool-down (5 minutes):**
- Gentle quad stretch
- Calf stretch
- Hip flexor stretch
- Deep breathing

### 8:00 AM - Post-Workout Recovery
**Choose one:**
- Smoothie with protein powder, banana, and spinach
- Greek yogurt with nuts and fruit
- Quinoa bowl with chickpeas and vegetables

### Throughout the Day - Nutrition Focus
**Lunch Ideas:**
- Lentil soup with whole grain bread
- Quinoa bowl with black beans and avocado
- Buddha bowl with roasted vegetables

**Snacks:**
- Hummus with carrot sticks
- Apple with almond butter
- Trail mix with nuts and dried fruit

**Dinner Ideas:**
- Stir-fried tofu with broccoli and brown rice
- Chickpea curry with quinoa
- Black bean and sweet potato tacos

### 9:00-10:00 PM - Evening Wind-Down Routine
- **No screens:** Put away phones, tablets, TV
- **Gentle movement:** 10 minutes of gentle yoga or stretching (focus on legs/hips)
- **Relaxing drink:** Chamomile or peppermint tea
- **Calming activity:** Reading or listening to soft music
- **Gratitude practice:** Write down 3 things you're grateful for
- **Pain management:** If knee pain present, apply heat/cold therapy for 15 minutes

- Blackout curtains or eye mask
- Knee support pillow if needed
- 5-10 minutes deep breathing exercises

## Week 1 Modifications

### If Knee Pain is High (7+)
- Skip squats and marching
- Focus on seated leg lifts and gentle stretches
- Extend cool-down to 10 minutes
- Apply ice after workout if swollen

### If Sleep Was Poor
- Add 5 extra minutes to morning meditation
- Include mid-day breathing break
- Earlier bedtime (9:30 PM)
- Extra attention to evening routine

### If Energy is Low
- Increase pre-workout snack size slightly
- Ensure adequate hydration throughout day
- Check if going to bed earlier helps

## Week 1 Success Metrics
- Complete at least 5 out of 7 exercise sessions
- Establish consistent sleep and wake times
- Implement evening routine 6 out of 7 nights
- Track all metrics for pattern identification
- Knee pain stays below 6/10 during exercise
- Begin to notice improvements in sleep quality by end of week

## Emergency Contacts & Resources
- If knee pain persists or worsens, consult healthcare provider
- Keep modification guidelines handy for high pain days
- Remember: consistency over intensity in Week 1
