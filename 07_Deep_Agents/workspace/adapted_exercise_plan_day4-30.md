# Adapted Exercise Plan (Days 4-30) - Knee-Friendly & Mobility Focused

## Key Adaptations Made:
- Focus on low-impact, knee-friendly exercises
- Daily mobility and flexibility work incorporated
- Progressive alternatives that maintain fitness goals
- Specific warm-up and cool-down routines for joint health

## Weekly Outline
- **Week 1 (Days 4-10): Introduction**
- **Week 2 (Days 11-17): Building Foundation**
- **Week 3 (Days 18-24): Increasing Intensity**
- **Week 4 (Days 25-30): Final Progression & Maintenance**

## Daily Exercise Plan

### Daily Warm-up Routine (10-15 minutes)
- **Gentle Marching in Place (2-3 mins)**
- **Arm Circles (1 min)**
- **Leg Swings (Side and Back - 1 min each leg)**
- **Heel Raises (1 min)**
- **Dynamic Hip Openers (1 min, slow and controlled)**

### Mobility & Flexibility Work (10-15 minutes daily)
- **Daily: 5-10 minutes of gentle stretching routines focusing on the hips, quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves.**
- **Suggested Stretches:**
  - Cat-Cow Stretch 
  - Supine Hamstring Stretch 
  - Standing Quadriceps Stretch 
  - Seated Forward Bend 
  - Gentle hip rotations

## Week 1: Introduction (Days 4-10)
**Strength & Stability: 3x/week (Days 4, 6, 8)**
1. Seated Leg Extensions (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
2. Wall Push-ups (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
3. Glute Bridges (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
4. Side-lying Leg Lifts (3 sets of 10-15 reps)

**Low-Impact Cardio: 2x/week (Days 5, 7)**
- Low-impact stationary cycling (15-25 mins at an easy pace)

## Week 2: Building Foundation (Days 11-17)
**Strength & Stability: 3x/week (Days 11, 13, 15)**
1. Standing Calf Raises (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
2. Modified Squats (sit back, keeping weight on heels - 3 sets of 10-15 reps)
3. Seated Rows with Resistance Band (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
4. Bird-Dog Exercise (3 sets of 10-12 reps each side)

**Low-Impact Cardio: 2x/week (Days 12, 14)**
- Swimming or water aerobics (20-30 mins)

## Week 3: Increasing Intensity (Days 18-24)
**Strength & Stability: 3x/week (Days 18, 20, 22)**
1. Step-ups (using a low platform, 3 sets of 8-12 reps each leg)
2. Modified Plank (on knees, 3x 20-30 seconds)
3. Side Leg Raises with Resistance Band (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
4. Bodyweight Deadlifts (focus on form, 3 sets of 10-15 reps)

**Low-Impact Cardio: 2x/week (Days 19, 21)**
- Elliptical machine (20-30 mins)

## Week 4: Final Progression & Maintenance (Days 25-30)
**Strength & Stability: 3x/week (Days 25, 27, 29)**
1. Single-leg Balance (hold for 20-30 seconds each leg; 3x)
2. Reverse Lunges (step back carefully, 3 sets of 8-10 reps each leg)
3. Modified Chest Press with Dumbbells (3 sets of 10-15 reps)
4. Clamshells with Resistance Band (3 sets of 10-15 reps each side)

**Low-Impact Cardio: 2x/week (Days 26, 28)**
- Dance-based fitness class (modify as needed to avoid strain) (20-30 mins)

## Cool-Down Routine (5-10 minutes)
- Gentle stretches similar to the daily flexibility work
- Focus on deep breathing and relaxation the last few minutes

## Important Notes:
- Adjust the number of sets and repetitions based on your comfort level
- Stay hydrated and monitor any knee discomfort
- Take rest days or active recovery (walking/yoga) as needed
- This plan emphasizes safety, mobility, and gradual progression while maintaining fitness goals
