# Alex's 2-Week Exercise Plan

## Program Overview
**Duration:** 2 weeks (6 total workouts)
**Schedule:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday
**Session Length:** 30 minutes each
**Focus:** Building consistency, improving energy, better sleep

## Workout Schedule
**Week 1:** Workouts 1, 2, 3 (Mon/Wed/Fri)
**Week 2:** Workouts 4, 5, 6 (Mon/Wed/Fri)

---

## STANDARD WARM-UP (5 minutes every session)
1. **Dynamic Stretching** (2 minutes)
   - Arm circles: 10 forward, 10 backward
   - Leg swings: 10 per leg (front/back, side to side)
   - Hip circles: 10 each direction
   - Neck rolls: 5 each direction

2. **Light Cardio** (3 minutes)
   - March in place with high knees: 1 minute
   - Gentle jogging in place: 1 minute
   - Side steps with arm swings: 1 minute

## STANDARD COOL-DOWN (5 minutes every session)
1. **Static Stretching** (4 minutes)
   - Quad stretch: 30 seconds each leg
   - Hamstring stretch: 30 seconds each leg
   - Calf stretch: 30 seconds each leg
   - Shoulder stretch: 30 seconds each arm
   - Chest stretch: 30 seconds
   - Spinal twist: 30 seconds each side

2. **Deep Breathing** (1 minute)
   - Box breathing: Inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4
   - Repeat 5-8 cycles
   - Focus on relaxation and gratitude

## WEEK 1 WORKOUTS

### WORKOUT 1 (Monday) - Foundation Building
**Main Session (20 minutes):**

**Circuit (repeat 3 times, 1-minute rest between rounds):**
1. **Bodyweight Squats** - 10-12 reps
   - Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
   - Lower as if sitting in a chair
   - Keep chest up, knees over toes

2. **Modified Push-ups** - 8-10 reps
   - Start with knee push-ups if needed
   - Keep straight line from head to knees/toes
   - Lower chest to floor, push back up

3. **Dumbbell Rows** (or water bottles) - 10 reps each arm
   - Bend forward slightly, pull weight to ribs
   - Squeeze shoulder blades together
   - Use 3-5 lb weights or water bottles

4. **Standing Calf Raises** - 12-15 reps
   - Rise onto toes, hold for 1 second
   - Lower slowly and controlled

5. **Plank Hold** - 20-30 seconds
   - Hold straight line from head to heels
   - Breathe normally, engage core

### WORKOUT 2 (Wednesday) - Strength & Flexibility
**Main Session (20 minutes):**

**Circuit (repeat 3 times, 1-minute rest between rounds):**
1. **Forward Lunges** - 10 reps per leg
   - Step forward, lower back knee toward floor
   - Keep front knee over ankle
   - Push back to starting position

2. **Glute Bridges** - 12-15 reps
   - Lie on back, feet flat, knees bent
   - Lift hips up, squeeze glutes
   - Hold for 1 second at top

3. **Side Lateral Raises** - 10 reps
   - Light weights (2-3 lbs) or water bottles
   - Raise arms out to sides to shoulder height
   - Lower slowly and controlled

4. **Bird-Dogs** - 8 reps per side
   - Start on hands and knees
   - Extend opposite arm and leg
   - Hold for 2 seconds, keep hips level

5. **Seated Forward Bend** - Hold 30 seconds
   - Sit with legs extended
   - Reach toward toes, breathe deeply
   - Focus on relaxation

### WORKOUT 3 (Friday) - Recovery & Core
**Main Session (20 minutes):**

**Relaxation Flow:**
1. **Cat-Cow Stretch** - 1 minute
   - Flow between arching and rounding spine
   - Breathe with movement
   - Focus on spinal mobility

2. **Child's Pose** - Hold 1 minute
   - Kneel and sit back on heels
   - Extend arms forward, rest forehead down
   - Focus on deep breathing

3. **Side Planks** - 20-30 seconds each side
   - Lie on side, prop up on forearm
   - Lift hips, hold straight line
   - Modify on knees if needed

4. **Gentle Yoga Flow** - 5 minutes

   - Repeat slowly 5 times
   - Focus on breath and mindfulness

5. **Meditation/Mindfulness** - 5 minutes
   - Comfortable seated position
   - Focus on breath or use guided meditation
   - End with gratitude for your body

## WEEK 2 WORKOUTS (Progressive Increase)

### WORKOUT 4 (Monday) - Power Building
**Main Session (20 minutes):**

**Circuit (repeat 3 times, 45-second rest between rounds):**
1. **Jump Squats** (or regular if joints sensitive) - 10 reps
   - Add small jump at top of squat
   - Land softly, immediately into next rep
   - Option: Regular squats with calf raise

2. **Incline Push-ups** - 10-12 reps
   - Hands on chair, couch, or steps
   - Lower chest to surface, push back up
   - Work toward standard push-ups

3. **Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows** - 10 reps each arm
   - Support one hand on chair
   - Pull weight up with free hand
   - Focus on squeezing back muscles

4. **Calf Raises with Slow Hold** - 12-15 reps
   - Rise onto toes, hold for 3 seconds
   - Lower very slowly (3-second count)

5. **Plank with Shoulder Taps** - 20 seconds
   - Hold plank position
   - Alternate tapping opposite shoulders
   - Keep hips stable

### WORKOUT 5 (Wednesday) - Total Body Strength
**Main Session (20 minutes):**

**Circuit (repeat 3 times, 45-second rest between rounds):**
1. **Step-ups** - 10 reps per leg
   - Use stairs or sturdy platform
   - Step up fully, control the descent
   - Focus on the working leg

2. **Deadlifts** (with weights or books) - 10-12 reps
   - Stand with weights in hands
   - Hinge at hips, lower weights to shins
   - Drive hips forward to stand

3. **Front Raises** - 10 reps
   - Light weights, arms straight
   - Raise forward to shoulder height
   - Lower slowly and controlled

4. **Plank Jacks** - 8-10 reps
   - Start in plank position
   - Jump feet apart and together
   - Keep core engaged throughout

5. **Lying Torso Twist** - Hold 30 seconds each side
   - Lie on back, knees bent
   - Lower knees to one side, hold
   - Keep shoulders on ground

### WORKOUT 6 (Friday) - Active Recovery & Celebration

2. **Seated Spinal Twists** - Hold 30 seconds each side
   - Sit cross-legged, twist gently
   - Use opposite hand for light leverage
   - Breathe deeply into the stretch

3. **Superman Exercise** - 10-12 reps
   - Lie face down, extend opposite arm and leg
   - Hold for 2 seconds, lower slowly
   - Strengthens lower back gently

4. **Deep Breathing Celebration** - 5 minutes
   - Comfortable position
   - Reflect on 2 weeks of progress
   - Set intentions for continued wellness

## EQUIPMENT ALTERNATIVES

**Don't have weights?**
- Water bottles (16 oz = ~1 lb)
- Soup cans (14 oz cans)
- Backpack with books
- Resistance bands

**Limited space?**
- All exercises can be done in 6x6 foot area
- Use wall for incline push-ups
- Use chair for step-ups and support

---

## ANXIETY-FRIENDLY MODIFICATIONS

1. **Start Slower:** Take longer rest periods if needed
2. **Focus on Breath:** Never hold your breath during exercise
3. **Listen to Your Body:** Stop if you feel overwhelmed
4. **End with Calm:** Always finish with relaxation/breathing
5. **Modify Intensity:** Reduce reps or intensity as needed

## ENERGY & SLEEP BENEFITS

**How This Program Boosts Energy:**
- Improves cardiovascular fitness
- Increases muscle strength and endurance
- Enhances mood through endorphin release
- Regulates blood sugar levels
- Builds confidence and mental resilience

**How This Program Improves Sleep:**
- Morning exercise helps regulate circadian rhythm
- Physical fatigue promotes deeper sleep
- Stress reduction through movement
- Friday relaxation sessions prepare weekend rest
- Regular routine supports consistent sleep schedule

## PROGRESSION BEYOND 2 WEEKS

After completing this program:
1. **Increase Frequency:** Add a 4th workout day
2. **Increase Duration:** Extend sessions to 35-40 minutes
3. **Increase Intensity:** Add more challenging exercise variations
4. **Add Variety:** Incorporate new activities (swimming, cycling, classes)
5. **Set New Goals:** Train for specific events or fitness milestones
