# Goran's 30-Day Joint-Friendly Exercise Program

## User Profile
- Enjoys hiking and outdoor activities
- Has occasional knee pain requiring joint-friendly exercises
- Goals: Stay active without aggravating knee, build strength and endurance for hiking
- Constraints: 30 minutes per day on weekdays, prefers morning activity
- Fitness level: Active but needs knee-safe modifications

## Program Structure
Progressive 4-week program focusing on hiking-specific strength and endurance while maintaining knee safety.

### Week 1: Foundation Building
**Daily Structure (30 minutes total):**
- **Warm-up (5 mins):** Gentle leg swings, arm circles, torso twists
- **Workout (20 mins):** 
  - Bodyweight squats (modified depth)
  - Wall push-ups
  - Clamshells for hip stability
  - Marching in place
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** Gentle stretching for major muscle groups

### Week 2: Building Strength
**Daily Structure (30 minutes total):**
- **Warm-up (5 mins):** Same as Week 1
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Step-ups (low height)
  - Seated leg lifts
  - Side leg raises
  - Walking (indoor/outdoor)
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** Same as Week 1

### Week 3: Endurance & Balance
**Daily Structure (30 minutes total):**
- **Warm-up (5 mins):** Same as Week 1
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Glute bridges
  - Modified lunges (partial range)
  - Heel-to-toe walk for balance
  - Low-impact cardio
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** Same as Week 1

### Week 4: Integration & Outdoor Activities
**Daily Structure (30 minutes total):**
- **Warm-up (5 mins):** Same as Week 1
- **Workout (20 mins):**
  - Combination of favorite exercises from previous weeks
  - Include outdoor walks when weather permits
  - Focus on hiking-specific movements
- **Cool-down (5 mins):** Same as Week 1

## Knee Pain Modification Guidelines
- **Pain Level 1-3 (mild):** Continue with modifications - reduce range of motion
- **Pain Level 4-6 (moderate):** Focus on seated exercises and gentle stretches
- **Pain Level 7+ (severe):** Rest day with gentle walking only
- **Always:** Stop if pain increases during exercise

## Equipment Needs (Minimal, Home-Friendly)
- Light dumbbells (optional, 2-5 lbs)
- Resistance bands (optional)
- Sturdy chair
- Low step (stairs work fine)
- Yoga mat (optional)

## Progress Tracking for Hiking Goals
- Keep daily exercise log with knee pain rating (1-10)
- Track walking distances/times during outdoor activities
- Weekly assessment of strength and endurance improvements
- Set specific hiking goals to achieve by day 30

## Morning Schedule Integration
- Best time: 6:00-6:30 AM (post-snack, pre-breakfast)
- Can be adapted for earlier if needed
- Include 5 minutes extra for transition between exercises
