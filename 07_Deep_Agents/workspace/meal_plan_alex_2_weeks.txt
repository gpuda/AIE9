# 2-Week Vegetarian Meal Plan for Alex

### Overview
This comprehensive meal plan is designed to improve energy levels, support better sleep, and help manage anxiety through a balanced vegetarian diet. It includes meal suggestions to aid in recovery after workouts, hydration tips, and meal prep suggestions.

## Week 1

### Daily Meal Plans
#### Day 1
**Breakfast:** Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and chia seeds  
**Snack 1:** Handful of mixed nuts  
**Lunch:** Quinoa salad with chickpeas, cucumbers, and lemon-tahini dressing  
**Snack 2:** Sliced apple with almond butter  
**Dinner:** Stir-fried tofu with broccoli and brown rice  

#### Day 2
**Breakfast:** Oatmeal topped with blueberries and walnuts  
**Snack 1:** Greek yogurt with honey  
**Lunch:** Vegetable wrap with hummus and mixed greens  
**Snack 2:** Baby carrots with tzatziki  
**Dinner:** Lentil soup with whole grain bread

#### Day 3
**Breakfast:** Overnight oats with banana and cinnamon  
**Snack 1:** Dark chocolate square  
**Lunch:** Spinach and feta stuffed peppers  
**Snack 2:** Herbal tea with a handful of trail mix  
**Dinner:** Chickpea curry with brown rice

#### Day 4
**Breakfast:** Whole grain toast with avocado and poached egg  
**Snack 1:** Pear slices with cheese  
**Lunch:** Rice bowl with black beans, corn, and salsa  
**Snack 2:** Cucumber slices with guacamole  
**Dinner:** Zucchini noodles with marinara sauce and lentil meatballs

#### Day 5
**Breakfast:** Smoothie bowl with assorted berries and granola  
**Snack 1:** Cinnamon roasted almonds  
**Lunch:** Caprese salad with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil  
**Snack 2:** Edamame  
**Dinner:** Butternut squash risotto

#### Day 6
**Breakfast:** Muesli with yogurt and fresh fruit  
**Snack 1:** Rice cakes with peanut butter  
**Lunch:** Grilled vegetable and pesto sandwich  
**Snack 2:** Orange slices  
**Dinner:** Sweet potato and black bean tacos

#### Day 7
**Breakfast:** Chia pudding with almond milk and mango  
**Snack 1:** Cottage cheese with pineapple  
**Lunch:** Broccoli and cheese quiche  
**Snack 2:** Mixed fruit salad  
**Dinner:** Pasta primavera with olive oil and Parmesan

#### Nutritional Explanations
- **Spinach:** Contains magnesium which helps improve sleep quality.
- **Nuts:** Provide healthy fats that stabilize blood sugar levels.
- **Chickpeas:** High in fiber, which aids digestion and boosts energy.
- **Oats:** Slow-release energy and high in melatonin, promoting sleep.
- **Bananas:** Contain tryptophan which helps with sleep.
- **Beans and lentils:** Great sources of protein, fiber, and iron for recovery.

### Hydration Recommendations
- Aim for 8-10 cups of water a day.  
- Avoid caffeine after 2 PM to improve sleep quality.

### Timing Suggestions
- **Pre-Workout:** Have a light snack 30-60 mins beforehand, like a banana or a small smoothie.  
- **Post-Workout:** Consume a meal or snack within 30 minutes after exercising for recovery, such as a protein smoothie or a meal with protein and carbs.

Feel free to adjust the meal options to fit your personal preferences. Happy eating!
